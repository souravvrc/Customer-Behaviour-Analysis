# 🛍️ Retail Customer Behavior & Shopping Trends Analysis

A complete end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes customer purchasing behavior and shopping trends using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**. The objective of this project is to transform raw retail data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, SQL-based business queries, interactive dashboards, and executive reporting.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate enormous amounts of customer transaction data every day. Analyzing this data helps organizations understand customer behavior, identify sales patterns, improve marketing strategies, and increase profitability.

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where customer shopping data is analyzed to answer key business questions and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-value customers.
- Discover top-performing product categories.
- Evaluate sales trends over time.
- Understand regional sales performance.
- Measure customer spending patterns.
- Generate business recommendations using data.
